Rebranding

UCL Hiking Club

Situation

When I became Marketing Officer of the UCL Hiking Club the club's corporate design was in a poor state. So I convinced the committee that we needed a rebranding.

Task

My goal was to create a consistent corporate design for the club. That included designing a new website, assets for social media, email templates, flyers and t-shirts.

Action

I started by brainstorming visuals that I associated with hiking. I also looked at existing outdoor logos to get inspired.

I then started drafting logos with the elements of my brainstorming ideas. I used Sketch to quickly generate alternative versions of my designs.

I went through multiple design iterations. I showed my first drafts to the committee to get feedback. I chose the most favoured logos, created multiple versions of each logo and gathered new feedback until we had the perfect logo.

Based on the logo I designed the website, social media assets, email template, flyers and t-shirts. I had to use Illustrator for the flyers and t-shirts because Sketch does not support CMYK.

  • 300 NEW MEMBERS - After my redesign the UCL Hiking Club has become a huge success in 2017/2018. The consistent design created brand recognition and a modern image. We grew the membership from 180 last year to almost 300 new members reflecting the success of my design.
  • The result can be seen on the website uclhiking.co.uk and on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram.
